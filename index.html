2023-06-07_Cycling Helmet

For my capstone project, I was interested in exploring the area of cyclist safety. I began searching for solutions for higher visibility, but I ultimately found more area for innovation in the form of active feedback systems. Through my research, I found that automobiles present the largest risk to cyclists, and electric vehicles are more likely to be involved in a cycling crash. My final design works to incorportate modern automobile safety features such as blind spot detection, haptic feedback, and brake lights. This system could give cyclists enhanced awareness of their surroundings and increase reaction time.

2023-03-14_Camp Stove

A compact and sturdy stove for car camping. Many of the current stove and windscreen designs are not optimized for more heavy duty cookware. By blocking the wind near the heat source, the workspace becomes more open and allows for the use of larger cookware.

2023-01-14_Nylon Paracord Bag

The cut and sew method of manufacturing can be wasteful due to the large amounts of offcuts. This project explores additive manufacturing. By using larger cord, the bag can be taken apart and the cord can be repurposed.
